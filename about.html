Our Journey

Children Impact and Development Initiative (CIDI) is a non-governmental, non-profit organization registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission since 2011. We are committed to nurturing the next generation by supporting children’s welfare and development. Mentally, socially, medically, and otherwise. We work with key stakeholders government, policy makers, communities, parents, and guardians to protect children’s rights and create opportunities for them to thrive. For over two decades, we have been shaping lives and preparing young people to become problem-solvers in society.

Our work is funded through book sales and voluntary support from family, friends, and partners. We believe you can be part of this vision to secure the future we all desire.
