Who We Are

Children Impact & Development Initiative (CIDI) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children through education, healthcare, and community support programs.

Since our founding, we've worked tirelessly to create sustainable change in communities across the region, ensuring every child has access to opportunities for a brighter future.

Our Programs

Education

Healthcare

Support
