Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How can I volunteer with CIDI?

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. You can apply through our Volunteer page, or contact our volunteer coordinator at childrenimpact@gmail.com. We offer both on-site and remote volunteer opportunities.

How are donations used?

90% of all donations go directly to program implementation. 7% covers administrative costs, and 3% goes toward fundraising. We publish annual financial reports for complete transparency.

Can I sponsor a specific child or project?

Yes! We offer both child sponsorship and project-specific funding options. Contact our partnerships team at childrenimpact@gmail.com for more information on targeted giving opportunities.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes, we accept various in-kind donations including educational materials, medical supplies, and clothing. Please contact us with details of your proposed donation so we can assess how best to utilize it.
